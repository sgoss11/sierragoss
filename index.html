About Me

Growing up in Washington State has helped me nurture my love for the outdoors. On warm summer weekends, I enjoy camping, hiking, paddle boarding and anything that will keep me in nature. In the fall and winter, you will find me painting, exploring new pumpkin patches and tree farms or curled up to read a good book. Helping others has always been a passion for me, from working as a neighborhood tutor and nanny for children k-12 and as a peer coach at UW where I helped young adults transition from high school to college life. I am always looking for a new community event or fundraiser to volunteer at and love being an active member in the community!

Karmic Designs is meant to provide local businesses with the means to share their products and services with the online community in an affordable and appealing way.
